# Application Notes For Water Treatment Chemicals

This file collects application-angle notes for water treatment chemical buyers and engineering teams.

## Textile Wastewater Color Reduction

PAC can be tested for textile wastewater color reduction when dyes and suspended matter can be destabilized through coagulation. Testing should record PAC dosage, pH, mixing conditions, settling time, sludge volume and clarified water quality.

Useful resources:

## Sludge Dewatering

PAM selection for sludge dewatering depends on sludge source, solids concentration, organic content, pH and equipment type. Charge type, charge density, dissolution quality, aging time and dosing point can change real performance.

Useful resources:

- [PAM selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pam-selection-guide/)

## Drinking Water Polishing

Activated carbon can be used for drinking water polishing to reduce taste, odor, residual organics and selected trace contaminants. Buyers should review iodine value, ash, moisture, hardness, particle size and document requirements.
