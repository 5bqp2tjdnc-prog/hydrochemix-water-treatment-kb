# Buyer Checklists

Use these checklists before comparing PAC, PAM, activated carbon or other water treatment chemicals only by price per ton.

## PAC Buyer Checklist

- Confirm target application: drinking water, wastewater, textile wastewater, pretreatment or sludge conditioning.
- Compare alumina content, basicity, insoluble matter and product form.
- Ask for specification sheet, COA and SDS/MSDS.
- Run jar testing with the real water sample.
- Compare total operating cost, not only unit price.

Resources:

- [PAC selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pac-selection-guide/)
- [Jar test guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/jar-test-guide/)

## PAM Buyer Checklist

- Confirm whether the target is clarification or sludge dewatering.
- Test anionic, cationic or nonionic PAM based on process needs.
- Check charge density, molecular weight range and dissolution behavior.
- Compare cake moisture, filtrate clarity and dosing rate.
- Confirm packaging, storage and safety documents.

Resources:

- [PAM selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pam-selection-guide/)
- [Polyacrylamide PAM product page](https://hydrochemix.com/en/products/polyacrylamide-pam/)

## Activated Carbon Buyer Checklist

- Confirm target contaminants and contact time.
- Compare coconut shell, coal-based, powdered and granular activated carbon.
- Review iodine value, moisture, ash, hardness and particle size.
- Check COA and SDS before purchase.
- Run pilot or column testing when the water source changes.
