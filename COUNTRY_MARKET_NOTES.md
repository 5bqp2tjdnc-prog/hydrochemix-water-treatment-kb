# Country Market Notes For Water Treatment Chemicals

This file collects country-angle notes for water treatment chemical buyers. It is intended as a public source document linked to the GitHub Pages mirror and the official HydroChemix knowledge base.

## Saudi Arabia PAC Buyers

Saudi Arabia water treatment buyers may evaluate PAC for municipal pretreatment, industrial water reuse, wastewater clarification and filtration pretreatment. Selection should consider water quality, pH, turbidity, salinity, alkalinity, basicity, alumina content and insoluble matter.

Useful resources:

- [PAC selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pac-selection-guide/)
- [Polyaluminium chloride PAC product page](https://hydrochemix.com/en/products/polyaluminium-chloride-pac/)
- [COA and SDS buying checklist](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/coa-sds-buying-checklist/)

## UAE Industrial Wastewater

UAE industrial wastewater projects may test PAC and PAM together because coagulation and flocculation need to work as one process step. Buyers should compare treated water clarity, sludge volume, cake moisture, dosing cost and process stability.

Useful resources:

- [PAC selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pac-selection-guide/)
- [PAM selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pam-selection-guide/)
- [Jar test guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/jar-test-guide/)

## Indonesia Textile Wastewater

Indonesia textile wastewater can include dyes, auxiliaries, salts, surfactants and suspended solids. PAC and PAM selection should be verified by jar testing and real wastewater samples instead of relying only on generic product grades.

Useful resources:

- [Water treatment knowledge base](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/)
- [PAC selection guide](https://hydrochemix.com/en/knowledge-base/pac-selection-guide/)
