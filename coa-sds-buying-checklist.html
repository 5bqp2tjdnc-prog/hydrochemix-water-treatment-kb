COA and SDS Buying Checklist

Before importing water treatment chemicals, buyers should check more than price. COA, SDS/MSDS, product specifications and packaging information reduce quality and logistics risks.

  • Specification sheet.
  • COA for batch quality values.
  • SDS/MSDS for safety and transport.
  • Packaging and pallet details.
  • Sample policy.
  • Export documents.
