Jar Test Checklist

A jar test is a practical screening method for comparing coagulants and flocculants before full-scale water treatment chemical dosing.

  1. Collect representative water or sludge sample.
  2. Record pH, turbidity, color and suspended solids.
  3. Prepare stock solutions.
  4. Test a dosage range.
  5. Compare floc formation, settling speed, clarity and sludge volume.
