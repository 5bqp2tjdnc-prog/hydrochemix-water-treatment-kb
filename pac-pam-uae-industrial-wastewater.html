PAC and PAM for UAE Industrial Wastewater Projects

In UAE industrial wastewater projects, PAC and PAM are often evaluated together because coagulation and flocculation need to work as one treatment step. PAC can destabilize suspended particles and colloids, while PAM helps form larger flocs for settling, flotation, or filtration.

The correct combination depends on wastewater source, oil content, TSS, COD load, pH, conductivity, and existing process design. A common mistake is selecting PAM only by molecular weight without checking charge type, dissolution method, and compatibility with the coagulant.

For project procurement, buyers should ask for product specifications, COA, SDS, recommended make-down concentration, and jar test support data where available. Site trials should monitor clarity, sludge volume, cake moisture, and chemical consumption per cubic meter.
