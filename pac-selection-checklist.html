PAC Selection Checklist for Water Treatment Buyers

Poly aluminium chloride, usually called PAC, is used for drinking water clarification, industrial wastewater treatment, textile wastewater color reduction and pretreatment systems.

Key checks

ItemBuyer note
Al2O3 contentCompare product grade and application requirement.
BasicityConfirm suitable range through jar testing.
Insoluble matterImportant for stricter water treatment applications.
ApplicationDrinking water and textile wastewater may need different grades.
DocumentsAsk for specification, COA and SDS/MSDS.
