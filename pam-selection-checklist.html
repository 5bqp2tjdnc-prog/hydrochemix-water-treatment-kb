PAM Selection Checklist

Polyacrylamide is used as a flocculant in sludge dewatering, wastewater clarification, mining wastewater, paper mill wastewater and textile wastewater.

Selection questions

  • Is the application clarification or sludge dewatering?
  • Should anionic, cationic or nonionic PAM be tested?
  • What charge density and molecular weight range are suitable?
  • Does the product dissolve cleanly without fisheyes?
  • What is the real dosage per ton of dry solids?
